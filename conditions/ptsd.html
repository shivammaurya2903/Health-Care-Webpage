Understanding PTSD

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) can develop after experiencing or witnessing a traumatic event. Recovery is possible with proper support and treatment.

What is PTSD?

PTSD is a mental health condition triggered by experiencing or witnessing a terrifying event. Symptoms may include flashbacks, nightmares, severe anxiety, and uncontrollable thoughts about the event.

Common Symptoms

  • Flashbacks or reliving the trauma
  • Nightmares
  • Avoidance of reminders
  • Hypervigilance
  • Emotional numbness
  • Difficulty sleeping

Treatment Options

Effective treatments include trauma-focused therapy, medication, support groups, and self-care strategies. Many people recover fully with appropriate treatment.

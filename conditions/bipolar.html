Understanding Bipolar Disorder

Bipolar disorder involves extreme mood swings from manic highs to depressive lows. With proper treatment and support, people can manage their symptoms and live fulfilling lives.

What is Bipolar Disorder?

Bipolar disorder is a mental health condition characterized by significant mood swings, including emotional highs (mania or hypomania) and lows (depression).

Common Symptoms

  • Manic episodes: Increased energy, euphoria, irritability
  • Depressive episodes: Sadness, hopelessness, low energy
  • Mixed episodes: Symptoms of both mania and depression
  • Rapid mood changes
  • Impulsive behavior
  • Sleep disturbances

Treatment Options

Treatment typically includes medication (mood stabilizers), therapy, lifestyle changes, and support from loved ones. Many people with bipolar disorder lead successful lives with proper management.

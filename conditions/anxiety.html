Understanding Anxiety

Anxiety is a normal reaction to stress, but when it becomes excessive, it can interfere with daily life. Learn about symptoms, causes, and treatment options.

What is Anxiety?

Anxiety disorders are the most common mental health conditions. They involve intense feelings of worry, fear, or nervousness that can make it hard to function in everyday situations.

Common Symptoms

  • Excessive worry or fear
  • Restlessness or feeling on edge
  • Rapid heartbeat
  • Sweating or trembling
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Sleep disturbances

Treatment Options

Effective treatments include therapy (like CBT), medication, lifestyle changes, and self-help strategies. Early intervention is key to managing anxiety effectively.
